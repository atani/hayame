# Privacy Policy - Hayame

**Last updated: 2026-02-14**

## Data Collection

Hayame does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

## Local Storage

Hayame uses `chrome.storage.local` solely to save your preferred playback speed (a single numeric value). This data:

- Is stored locally on your device only
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Is never shared with any third party
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions

- **storage**: Used to persist your preferred playback speed across sessions
- **Host permissions** (`<all_urls>`): Required to detect and control HTML5 video elements on any website. The extension only interacts with `<video>` elements and does not read or modify other page content.

## Third-Party Services

Hayame does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [github.com/atani/hayame](https://github.com/atani/hayame/issues).
